The Woven Book is a free browser game where you can write a story with others.

Gain influence points by writing and use them to affect the story and develop your character. You can also participate in encounters, where you will play against other players. Create an effective build, learn how to wirte stories, train your memeory, typing skills and read interesting stories.

✤ RPG style character progression (classes, levels, skills, items, talents etc.)
✤ Achievements linked to writing performance.
✤ Leaderboard for best writers in the game.

The Book

Players choose a storyline written by other player and continues it. Best ideas are upvoted and get to the top of the chapter. In this way the story continues and most interesting autors gain influence points. Influence points are the curreny of the game, used to vote for storylines, buy items, consumables etc.

Classes

Each class has a unique playstyle, for example Alchemist can craft consumables that allow him to gain buffs and heal himself. He also can use spells like, Plague which will swap characters in your oponents text making him slower during the encounter.

Warlord, on the other hand, will focus on brute force and deal extra damange with each typed character.

Encounters

During encounters you will play against other players. You will be given a text, in size equal to oponent life points. Both players will start typing, dealing damage equal to amount of characters typed.

The outcome of the duel will be affected by stats, cllass skills, items, players typing speed, memory and precision.
